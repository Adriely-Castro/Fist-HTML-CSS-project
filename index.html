Who is BLACKPINK?

Everything You Need To Know About The K-Pop Girl Group

BLACKPINK's in your area — and they're a force to be reckoned with. The four-member K-pop girl group hit the top of the charts with their infectious singing, rapping, and dancing after their debut The Album debuted in 2020. Hailing from Seoul, Korea, BLACKPINK became the first K-pop girl act to perform a set at Coachella and they were even ranked as the highest charting K-pop girl group on the Billboard Hot 100 and Billboard 200. Pretty much everything these K-pop superstars touch turns into gold, leading to successful solo efforts and collaborations with massive brands. No matter if you're a BLINK, or you're just getting familiar with BLACKPINK after catching them on the red carpet at the 2022 MTV VMAs, there's so much to learn about BLACKPINK. Ahead, find everything you need to know about everyone's favorite K-pop girl group, BLACKPINK.


The group's solo projects are equally epic.

Blackpink’s Jennie continue to make history with her debut track, Solo. The track crossed over 800 million views on YouTube, establishing her as the first female K-pop star to hit that milestone. Jennie had released the song in 2018 on November 12.

In April 2021, Rosé became the first artist to reach number one on a Billboard Global chart as a soloist and as part of a group with the release of her single, "On The Ground."The music video was released on the same day and became the most viewed YouTube music video in 24 hours by a solo K-pop artist after bringing in 41.6 million views.
